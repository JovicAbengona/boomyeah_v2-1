      This is the Backend Team which is part of the Engineering Team. They are responsible for all the Backend processes of our projects like Coding Dojo, DataCompass, and Focus Tracker. They specialize in numerous technologies like HTML/CSS, LESS, SASS, JavaScript, React/React Native, and many more.
